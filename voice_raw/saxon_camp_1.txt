CERDIC: Don't touch their women. We don't mix with these people. What kind of offspring do you think that would yield? Weak people. Half people. I will not have our Saxon blood watered down by mixing with them.

WOULD-BE RAPIST: According to our laws, no man may deny me the spoils of our conquest!

CERDIC: Are you challenging me? If you want to challenge me, you have to have a sword in your hand. As long as my heart beats, I rule and you hold your tongue. Or I'll cut it out.

CERDIC: Why should I trust you? You're a traitor to your own people.

CERDIC: I'll attack from the north with the main army. You bring your men down here. Cut off their retreat to the south. Burn every village. Never leave behind you a man, woman or child who can ever carry a sword.
